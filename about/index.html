Thiago Moraes

About

Name’s Thiago, Brazilian name that translates to James or Jacob depending on who you ask.

I’m a Computer Engineer from Brazil. Over the last few years, I’ve often worked in positions supporting Latin America in a mix of Software Developer, Consultant and Sales Engineer. It’s funny how I loved the “hardcore” stuff while in college and end up to be more excited to work in places where technology is a means to an end.

In my life, I’ve moved all around:

  • São Mateus do Sul and Curitiba, Paraná, during my infancy and most of high school
  • São José dos Campos, São Paulo, senior year in high school
  • São Carlos, SP and Santa Fé, Argentina - college at UFSCar and UNL
  • São Paulo, SP and Campinas, SP - six years working in the tech industry
  • Bay Area - where I’ve been since mid-2018

I love to travel and have already visited almost 20 countries in 3 continents. While I’ve never worked in the Financial Industry, I have a big interest in Financial Markets, especially how technology influences it.

Despite my love for travel, I’ve never got used to sleep on planes. My Nintendo Switch has been my best friend on long-haul flights. In my high school days I played on a bunch of Table Tennis tournaments and still pick-up my paddle every now and then. I can play in the office and not be ashamed of myself, but not a lot more.

Currently, I work at Facebook, where I work on WhatsApp Business Products. Opinions here are my own.
